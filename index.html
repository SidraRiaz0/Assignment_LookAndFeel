ABOUT US

WE ARE
CHANGEMAKERS

At a time where gaming culture is evolving, we know that women are the driving force behind innovation and creativity. Female gamers aren't just playing games, they're creating worlds, leading communities, and breaking barriers in real time. We help developers, publishers, and brands recognize and celebrate the contributions of women in gaming.

Many well-known and beloved games have been developed or significantly influenced by women. Notable examples include Uncharted, Journey, Tomb Raider, Centipede, and Portal, among others. Women have also contributed to the creation of games like Animal Crossing: New Horizons, BioShock, Child of Light, Cities: Skylines, and Celeste. Furthermore, many indie games and board games have been developed by women, such as Unpacking, Flowerpower, and Dog Park.
